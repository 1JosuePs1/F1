FORMULA 1: 2022

2022 Races

Max Verstappen won the Formula One World Championship in a season where his team, Red Bull, also won the constructors' title. Verstappen set a historic record of 15 wins in a single season, and his teammate Sergio Perez finished third. Carlos Sainz won his first race in the category with Ferrari.
